📍 How to Access the Extension

🔍 Look for the extension icon in your browser toolbar
It's located in the top-right corner of Chrome, next to the address bar.

Click the puzzle piece icon (🧩) if you don't see it, then pin "Worth My Time" for easy access.

To use the extension:

  1. Click the extension icon in your browser toolbar
  2. Make sure "Enable conversion" is turned ON (green)
  3. Visit any shopping website
  4. Prices will automatically show work time! ⏱️
💡 Note: We don't convert between currencies. Works best on sites using your local currency.
